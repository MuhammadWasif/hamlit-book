About Joy

Joy transforms your "new tab" page. Each time you open a tab, you will be greeted with an incredible new tab filled with custom widgets, new book suggestions, and a couple of banner ads. We collect ad revenue from the page and use it to donate books for kids.

Spread the joy of reading and raise money for book donation with every tab you open in Chrome! Just by surfing the web, you can build libraries and assist child education for children in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support child education. Join us by adding the extension now and spread the joy of reading, learning, and sharing books. We will be adding new child support causes and more options to raise money in the future.

Your Privacy Matters

Joy is committed to protecting your privacy and using your data responsibly. We save information relevant to your profile on Joy (such as your preferences and new tab page widgets). We do not collect any information we do not need.

The new tab page contains advertisements that help us raise money for charity. These ads are delivered by our advertising partners. These partners may collect anonymized data about you in order to show you ads you'll be interested in.
